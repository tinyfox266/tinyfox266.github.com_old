The definition of the coodinate system of android

the origin is left_down corner of the phone

x axis points to the right side of the phone

y axis points to the top of the phone

z axis points to the front of the screen

The value returned by the accelerator is as follows:

  • value[0]:acceleration on x-axis minus Gx
  • value[1]: acceleration on y-axis minus Gy
  • value[2]: acceleration on z-axis minus Gz

Gx(Gy/Gz) represents the gravity acceleration on x-axis(y-axis/z-axis)

When the phone is static, then the sum of the squares of value[0], value[1], value[2] is the square of the gravity acceleration G^2.

Computation of the angle between each axis and the vertical direction

let x=value[0], y=value[1], z=value[2]

angleXG = x/ sqrt(x2+y2+z^2)

angleYG = y/ sqrt(x2+y2+z^2)

angleZG = z/ sqrt(x2+y2+z^2)
