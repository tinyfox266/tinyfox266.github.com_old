• flv to mp3
    mencoder -oac mp3lame -ovc copy -of rawaudio -o <file.mp3> <file.flv>
  • combine flvs
    mencoder -oac pcm -ovc copy -idx -o <file.flv> <flv_to_be_combined>
  • extract audio from video mencoder -oac mp3lame -ovc frameno -ss <starttime> -endpos <endtime> -o <output.mp3> -of rawaudio <input_videos>
  • extract one part from the video mencoder -oac mp3lame -ovc lavc -ss <starttime> -endpos <endtime> -o <output> <input_videos>
