• Definition
    • RAID means Redundant Array of Independent Disks, it is a storage technology that combines disk drive components into a logical unit.
  • Standard levels
    • RAId 0 block-level stripping without parity or mirroring.
    • RAID 1 mirroring without parity or stripping.
    • RAID 2 bit-level stripping with dedicated Hamming-code parity.
    • RAID 3 byte-level stripping with dedicated parity.
    • RAID 4 block-level stripping with dedicated parity.
    • RAID 5 block-level stripping with distributed parity.
    • RAID 6 block-level stripping with double distributed parity.
